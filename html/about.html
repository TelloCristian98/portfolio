My Story starts here!

I'm a web developer and software engineer from Quito, Ecuador. I have a passion for creating web systems and applications that improve people's daily lives. My specialty is in backend development using technologies like PHP, Laravel, NodeJS and Ajax. I also have extensive experience with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Currently, I'm expanding my frontend skills by learning ReactJS. As a software engineering student at Universidad de las Fuerzas Armadas ESPE, I've worked on projects like updating my university's website using WordPress.

I'm also a research assistant in the computer science department. In addition to software engineering, I have two years of experience studying electronic engineering and enjoy working on IoT projects. I also completed an intensive ESL program at Atlantic Cape Community College in the US, so I'm a certified fluent English speaker and writer. My goal is to continue growing as a developer and building innovative solutions that make a difference.

In my free time, I love staying active by playing soccer every weekend and going to the gym during the weekdays. I also play guitar in a rock-pop band, which allows me to pursue my musical passions.
